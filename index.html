About Us

Established in 2006 by renowned chef Suyog Jain, Spicy Mahal has been a cornerstone of Pune's culinary scene, renowned for its exquisite Indian vegetarian cuisine. From its inception, Spicy Mahal has been dedicated to showcasing the rich diversity of flavors found across India's regions, offering a menu that highlights traditional dishes such as fragrant Biryanis, flavorful Paneer Tikka, and hearty Daal Makhani.
